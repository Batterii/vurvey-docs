# Introduction to Vurvey

Welcome to Vurvey - the AI-powered consumer insights platform that combines artificial intelligence with real human insights through video surveys.

## What is Vurvey?

Vurvey transforms traditional market research by:

- **AI Agents** - Deploy AI personas that represent different consumer viewpoints
- **Video Surveys** - Collect rich, qualitative data through video responses
- **Rapid Insights** - Turn weeks of research into hours using AI analysis
- **Real Humans** - Combine AI with a network of 3+ million real consumers

## Platform Overview

After logging in, you'll have access to these main sections:

| Section | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| **Home** | AI chat interface for conversational research |
| **Agents** | Manage AI personas (Consumer Personas) |
| **People** | Manage audiences and populations |
| **Campaigns** | Create and manage research surveys |
| **Datasets** | Organize files and data for AI analysis |
| **Workflow** | Automate multi-step research processes (Beta) |
